Poem

There was a bright Shinning star, that led 3 wise man .

To the birth of the king, who become a carpenter.

A fisher of men and had 12 disciples , on a dark day , full of envy pain and death, he was crucified.

He shed his precious blood, so we would become white as snows.

He is now seated in Heaven, as the Father, son and Holy Spirit.

He did all this because he loves you.
